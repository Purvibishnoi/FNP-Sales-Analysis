
# FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes 1,000 sales orders from **Ferns N Petals (FNP)** to identify key business insights across revenue, customer spending, product categories, and delivery performance. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making for improving sales, marketing, and logistics.

---

## Key Highlights
- Total Revenue: ₹35,20,984  
- Total Orders: 1,000  
- Average Spend per Order: ₹3,520.98  
- Average Delivery Time: 5.53 days  

**Top Findings**
- Highest revenue from Anniversaries and Raksha Bandhan.  
- Best-performing product categories: Colors and Soft Toys.  
- Peak sales in February and March (Valentine’s Day & Holi).  
- Major order volume from Dhanbad and Imphal.  

---

## Recommendations
- Reduce delivery times for time-sensitive occasions.  
- Strengthen Diwali campaigns with targeted promotions.  
- Focus ads around peak hours (12 PM & 6 PM).  
- Expand regional marketing in high-performing cities.
